Our Tours

Select the best one for you based on days and intensity.


Explorer

Explore the wonders of Antarctica on our 10-day Explorer Tour, a soft and immersive experience. Delve into calm excursions focused on raising awareness about Antarctica and providing educational insights. Discover the serene beauty of the icy landscapes while participating in thoughtful activities designed to enhance your understanding of this unique ecosystem.

Warrior

Embark on a 15-day adventure with our Warrior Tour, an intense exploration of Antarctica. This expedition involves extended walks on the ice, offering a more vigorous experience for those seeking a thrilling adventure. Traverse the frozen landscapes, engage in challenging activities, and absorb the breathtaking scenery as you push the boundaries of exploration. This tour is designed for the adventurous souls ready to embrace the wild side of Antarctica.
